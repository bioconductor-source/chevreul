# Pathway Enrichment Analysis 

Pathway enrichment is determined based on marker genes. 

Reference pathway datasets include: 
1. 'GO_Biological_Process_2018',
2. 'GO_Cellular_Component_2018',
3. 'GO_Molecular_Function_2018',
4. 'KEGG_2016',
5. 'WikiPathways_2016',
6. 'Reactome_2016',
7. 'Panther_2016',
8. 'Human_Gene_Atlas',
9. 'Mouse_Gene_Atlas'
